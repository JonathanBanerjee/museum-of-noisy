ABOUT NOISY

Noisy is a black cat who always ensures his voice is heard. He is in charge of the family and let's the family know when it's snack time, time to get outside and when it's time to rest. He is currently seventeen years old and he was previously known as "Shadow" by his previous family.

VISITING THE GALLERY OF NOISY

Noisy usually has visiting hours between 6AM to 2PM. However, as he's in charge of the gallery, he decides the opening hours which may change on a whim. If he decides it's nap time, the gallery will be closed for the day.

TICKETS FOR THE GALLERY OF NOISY

Tickets to the gallery of Noisy are available. However, they can only be purchased in the form of Dreamies or Creamy treats (as per his request). If there are any further questions please send an email to thenoisygallery@notarealemail.com
